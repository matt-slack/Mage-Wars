Additional Notes

Create a Target on a Card:
  • You can target a card by holding shift and left-clicking on it
  • You clear the target the same way
  • You can have multiple cards targeted
Create an Arrow from one card to another:
  • You can create an arrow pointing from one card to another by holding Shift+Left-click on the source card, then drag your mouse to the destination card
  • Clear the arrow by shift+left-clicking on the source card again
  • Pro Tip: Use this to point out which card you want to attach your Enchantment to and you will be asked to pay for the casting cost.
